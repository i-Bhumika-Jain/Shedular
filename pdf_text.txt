Reality check for weight goal
80 kg to 50 kg in 2 months (30 kg in 8 weeks) is not safe. A healthy pace is 0.5-1 kg/week. So aim for
about 72-74 kg in 8 weeks, then 55-60 kg in 6-8 months. Slow loss protects skin, hair, mood, and
hormones.

Clear skin plan (underarms + stretch marks + tan)
This version is safer than daily harsh scrubbing. Most DIY (lemon, sugar, baking soda) can irritate
and make darkness worse.
1) Face skin - simple daily routine
Morning: gentle face wash -> moisturizer -> sunscreen SPF 30+.
Night: cleanse -> moisturizer. If you use actives (niacinamide/retinol), use only at night.
2) Underarm darkness (safe schedule)
Main causes: friction, shaving irritation, sweat + dead skin. If darkness is thick/velvety, check fasting
sugar once (sometimes related to insulin resistance).
Day
Night routine (underarms)
Daily
Aloe vera gel (thin layer) OR a gentle moisturizer (keep area dry).
Mon + Thu
Gentle exfoliation: lactic acid 5% lotion (very small amount).
Tue + Fri
Niacinamide (4-5%) lotion/serum (if it suits).
Avoid
No daily scrubbing, no lemon + sugar daily, no baking soda. Patch test everything.
3) Stretch marks (stomach) - fade plan
Stretch marks usually fade 60-80% with consistency. Slow weight loss + hydration + massage help.
Daily: body oil or thick moisturizer after bath + 2-3 min massage on stretch mark area.
3 nights/week: retinol body lotion/cream (if not pregnant; stop if irritation). Follow with moisturizer.
Nutrition support: protein daily + vitamin C fruit (amla/guava/orange).
4) Tan on arms/face - remove and prevent
Non-negotiable: sunscreen on face + exposed arms every morning. Reapply if you are in sun.
2x/week pack: besan + curd (or milk) + pinch turmeric for 10-12 min.
1x/week pack: tomato + curd for 8-10 min.
Avoid rubbing ice directly for long; wrap in cloth.
